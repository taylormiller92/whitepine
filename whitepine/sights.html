Things to Do

Camping

There are many campsites to choose from at White Pine. We have campsites ranging from small to large.


Hiking and Trails

Enjoy the many trails there are to explore. They range from a leisurely pace to intermediate. See our FAQ section for where to go, or any questions you may have about our trails.


Canoeing and Kayaking

Get out on the water and enjoy the rivers and creeks. Check the local weather alerts and updates to find the best time.
