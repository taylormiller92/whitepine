Recent Bear Sighting

December 7th, 2018

White Pine has been a prime location for individuals and families seeking the great outdoors. Going on for miles, White Pine has many activities. You can go hiking on one of our many trails, as well as kayaking or canoeing down the river. There are many animals to see as well. Bears are known to be in the area. Be wary when crossing paths with these animals, as they are not to be disturbed in their natural habitat. Recently, a family of Black Bears were spotted. Please see our FAQ on how to deal with these encounters.
